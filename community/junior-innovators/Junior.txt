Junior Innovators Challenge (JIK)

Organization: Junior Innovators Challenge
Date: August 2025 (1 Month)

I volunteered as part of the Multimedia Division at the Junior Innovators Challenge (JIK), an educational event that brings together young innovators to showcase creativity, problem-solving abilities, and forward-thinking ideas across various domains. The competition aims to cultivate innovation mindsets among youth and provide them with a platform to present ideas that address real-world challenges.

My primary responsibility was to capture and document key moments throughout the event through photography and video. This included recording project presentations, team interactions, critical competition moments, and behind-the-scenes efforts that reflected the energy and commitment of the participants. This experience strengthened my appreciation for storytelling through media, collaborative teamwork, and the importance of showcasing innovation to inspire others. Witnessing the enthusiasm, imagination, and determination of young participants was deeply motivating and reinforced my belief in youth-driven innovation as a powerful force for shaping the future.
