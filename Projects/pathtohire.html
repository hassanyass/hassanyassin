    EdTech & Career Development

    PathToHire

    AI-Powered Mock Interview Platform & Career Coach

    Python Flask JavaScript RapidAPI Generative AI

    The Problem

    Countless talented candidates fail to land jobs not because they lack the technical skills, but because they lack interview practice.

    Generic interview prep is ineffective because it doesn't account for a candidate's specific seniority level, tech stack, or industry.

    The Solution

    PathToHire is a personalized AI companion that democratizes high-quality interview coaching.

    Built during the SalamHack Hackathon, the platform ingests a user's CV and career goals to generate hyper-realistic, fully customized interview simulations with real-time feedback.

    The Backend Pipeline

    Dynamic Ingestion

    The backend parses the candidate's CV data to understand their exact profile, experience level, and technical stack.

    Generative Questioning

    Using RapidAPI and Hugging Face LLM models, the system generates adaptive technical and behavioral questions tailored to the user's profile.

    Evaluation Engine

    The AI evaluates the candidate's responses in real-time and delivers structured, constructive feedback on areas for improvement.

    "We learned more from our countless failed experiments than we did from our successes."

    Engineering Resilience

    During this hackathon, our team faced countless failed API integrations—from wrestling with RapidAPI endpoints to fine-tuning Hugging Face models and navigating PythonAnywhere deployment hurdles. These challenges resulted in a robust, functional backend architecture.

    Team & Recognition

    Developed for the SalamHack Hackathon, this project was brought to life alongside my dedicated engineering partners, Fadul Osman and Mohamed Doma. This MVP is just the beginning of a larger vision to help job seekers succeed. =======
