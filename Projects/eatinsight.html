    The Problem

    Maintaining a healthy diet requires tracking macro-nutrients, but manual logging is tedious and error-prone. People often forget to log meals or struggle to estimate portion sizes accurately.

    The Solution

    EatInSight is a Telegram bot that serves as a frictionless bridge between your eating habits and health data. Simply send a picture of your meal, and the AI analyzes it to extract macros and logs them directly to your Notion Calendar.

    How It Works

    1. Send a Photo

    Take a picture of your meal and send it to the @Foodie_mvp_bot on Telegram.

    2. AI Analysis

    The bot uses OpenAI Vision to analyze the image and extract nutritional information (carbs, protein, fats, calories).

    3. Auto-Logging

    n8n automation workflow adds the data to your Notion Calendar so you can track your eating habits over time.

    "Your food, Your data, Your decision."

    This project was a turning point in my understanding of automation and n8n workflows, transforming what I initially dismissed as hype into a powerful tool for real-world impact.
