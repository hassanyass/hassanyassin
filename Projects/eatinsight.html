EatInSight

Telegram bot that analyzes food photos to extract macros and nutrition data. 2nd place winner at Melaka AI Hackathon for revolutionizing dietary tracking.

2nd Place Melaka AI Hackathon
n8n · OpenAI Vision · Notion Tech Stack

EatInSight is an AI-powered Telegram bot that enables users to upload a photo of their meal and receive an analysis of macronutrients and nutritional content. The system uses OpenAI Vision for image understanding and automatically logs results into a Notion calendar for effortless dietary tracking.

The automation pipeline was built with n8n, showcasing how AI can integrate seamlessly into existing productivity tools. The project won 2nd place at the Melaka AI Hackathon, highlighting its impact on health tech and user-centric automation.
