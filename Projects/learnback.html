    LearnBack

    AI Learning-by-Teaching Platform

    Python Generative AI NLP JavaScript

    The Problem

    Modern self-directed learning relies heavily on passive revision methods such as reading slides, notes, or watching videos. These approaches often fail to reveal misunderstandings, give learners a false sense of mastery, and lack immediate, personalized feedback.

    LearnBack addresses this gap by turning revision into an interactive teaching exercise, where understanding is continuously tested and refined.

    The Solution

    LearnBack is an AI-driven learning platform designed to improve conceptual understanding by reversing the traditional learning flow. Instead of passively consuming content, learners actively teach concepts back to the system, allowing the AI to evaluate understanding, detect misconceptions, and provide targeted feedback.

    The platform focuses on deep learning, not memorization, by encouraging explanation, reasoning, and reflection.

    How It Works

    1. Concept Selection

    The learner selects a topic or concept they want to revise.

    2. Teach-Back Interaction

    The learner explains the concept in their own words. The system treats the learner as the "teacher."

    3. AI Analysis

    NLP and generative AI analyze concept coverage, logical structure, accuracy, and detect misconceptions.

    4. Adaptive Feedback

    The AI responds with clarifying questions, corrections, simplified explanations, and suggestions on what to revisit.

    Key Features

    Learning-by-Teaching

    AI-based misconception detection with adaptive feedback instead of static answers.

    Conceptual Depth

    Analyzes not just correctness but conceptual depth and understanding.

    Self-Study Ready

    Designed for self-study, exam revision, and concept mastery.

    Project Vision

    LearnBack aims to become a personal AI study partner that helps learners think clearly, explain confidently, and truly understand what they study—by making teaching the core learning activity.
